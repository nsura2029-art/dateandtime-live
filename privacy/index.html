1. Summary in plain English

The short version: We don't collect personal data unless you give it to us. We don't sell your data. We use Cloudflare for hosting (which logs basic request metadata). You can use this site with zero cookies if you want.

If you scroll down, you'll find the full legal version covering GDPR (EU), UK PECR, LGPD (Brazil), CCPA/CPRA (California), PIPEDA/Quebec Law 25 (Canada), and other applicable laws.

2. What data we collect

Automatically, when you visit the site:

  • IP address (used to detect your approximate location for the clock, never stored with personal identifiers)
  • Approximate geolocation (city, region, country — derived from your IP by Cloudflare)
  • Browser & device info (user agent, screen size — for responsive design)
  • Request metadata (timestamp, URL requested, HTTP status — for error tracking)

Only if you give it to us:

  • Feedback you submit via the in-app feedback prompt (stored in our database, no PII required)
  • Email address if you write to hello@dateandtime.live
  • Cookie preferences (essential, analytics opt-in, advertising opt-in)

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your name (unless you tell us)
  • Your real address
  • Your phone number
  • Biometric data
  • Health, financial, or other "special category" data

3. How we use the data

  • To show you the current time in your city (the main purpose)
  • To respond to your feedback or support requests
  • To fix bugs (anonymized error logs)
  • To improve the site (only if you opt in to analytics — see Cookies)

We do not use the data for: advertising targeting, profiling, automated decision-making that affects you, or any commercial purpose beyond keeping the site running.

4. Cookies & local storage

We use one cookie: cookie_consent. It remembers your cookie preferences (Essential only / Accept all / Customize). It does not track you across sites.

Our local storage usage:

  • tdl-home — your "home" city for the world clock (stays on your device)
  • tdl-favs — your favorite cities (stays on your device)
  • tdl-theme — light or dark mode (stays on your device)
  • tdl-format — 12h or 24h clock format (stays on your device)

No third-party analytics or ad cookies are set without your consent. You can change your preferences anytime via the "Cookies" link in the footer.

5. Sharing your data

We share data with exactly three service providers, all of which are bound by data processing agreements:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, DDoS protection (privacy policy at cloudflare.com)
  • Nager.Date — public holiday data API (we fetch from them; they don't get your data)
  • Wikipedia — on-this-day events (same — we fetch, they don't get your data)

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers unless you explicitly opt in.

6. Data retention

  • Server logs: 30 days, then deleted (Cloudflare's default)
  • Feedback submissions: until you ask us to delete them, or 2 years, whichever comes first
  • Cookie consent record: 12 months, then we re-prompt
  • Email correspondence: until you ask us to delete, or 2 years of inactivity

7. Your rights (general)

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we have about you
  • Ask us to correct it
  • Ask us to delete it
  • Opt out of any processing (where applicable)

To exercise any of these, email hello@dateandtime.live. We respond within 30 days.

8. Jurisdiction-specific rights

🇪🇺 🇬🇧 If you're in the EU or UK (GDPR / UK GDPR / PECR)

You have the right to: access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction of processing, data portability, object to processing, withdraw consent at any time, lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our lawful basis for processing is your consent (for analytics/ads) and legitimate interest (for essential site operation).

🇺🇸 If you're in California (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to: know, delete, correct, opt out of sale/sharing, limit use of sensitive personal information, non-discrimination. We do not sell your personal information. See the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link in our footer.

🇧🇷 If you're in Brazil (LGPD)

You have the right to: confirmation of existence, access, correction, anonymization, portability, deletion, consent withdrawal, complaint with the ANPD. The lawful bases for processing are consent and legitimate interest.

🇨🇦 If you're in Canada (PIPEDA / Quebec Law 25)

You have the right to: access, correction, withdraw consent, complain to the OPC (or the CAI in Quebec). We comply with Canadian anti-spam law (CASL) for any commercial communications.

🇦🇺 If you're in Australia (Privacy Act)

You have the right to: know, access, correct, complain to the OAIC. We comply with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • All traffic is HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
  • Hosted on Cloudflare's global network (DDoS protection, WAF)
  • No third-party trackers by default
  • Minimal data collection (data minimization principle)

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a data breach affecting you, we'll notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

10. Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us and we'll delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. If the changes are material, we'll show a notice on the homepage for 30 days.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or complaints:

Email: hello@dateandtime.live

We aim to respond within 30 days. If you're not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
