🚀 Space · Importance 5

Moon landing — Apollo 11 lands on the Moon

Apollo 11 was the spaceflight that first landed humans on the Moon. Commander Neil Armstrong and lunar module pilot Buzz Aldrin landed the Apollo Lunar Module Eagle on July 20, 1969. Armstrong became the first person to step onto the Moon's surface, followed by Aldrin. They spent about 2 hours and 32 minutes on the lunar surface.
