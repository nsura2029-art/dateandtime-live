Frequently asked questions

What time is it in my city?

dateandtime.live auto-detects your city and shows the current time in the big clock. You can also search any of 33,945 cities worldwide. The clock updates every second and shows the IANA time zone, UTC offset, and DST status.

How do I find the time difference between two cities?

Add both cities to your favorites rail. Click one to show its time in the big clock; the other stays visible in the rail. The UTC offset of each card tells you the difference.

Is this site free?

Yes. dateandtime.live is free, with no signup. We don’t sell your data. Optional advertising keeps the site free (only shown if you opt in to advertising cookies).

How accurate is the time?

The time is computed in your browser using JavaScript's Intl.DateTimeFormat API (IANA Time Zone Database). We cross-check against our server time to within ~50ms. For safety-critical timing, consult an official source (NIST, USNO).

Do you have data for my country?

We cover 33,945 cities in 242 countries, 408 IANA time zones, and 1,600+ public holidays across 39 countries. If something is missing, use the feedback button and we'll add it.

How do I report an error?

Click the feedback button at the bottom-right of any page, or email hello@dateandtime.live. We aim to fix critical errors within 7 days.

Can I use this site commercially?

Yes. You may use the site for personal or commercial purposes. The data is sourced from public-domain and open-license sources (IANA, GeoNames, Nager.Date, Wikipedia). See our Editorial Policy for details.

Does the site work offline?

After your first visit, much of the site works offline. We use Cloudflare's edge cache and have minimal JS dependencies.
