About Bangladesh

People's Republic of Bangladesh is located in AS (Asia). The country spans 147,570 km² and has a population of approximately 36,396,047 across 137 tracked cities.

  • Country codes: BD (ISO 3166-1 alpha-2), BGD (alpha-3)
  • Capital: Dhaka
  • Phone code: +880
  • Currency: Bangladeshi taka (৳)
  • Languages: Bengali

Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Bangladesh have?

Bangladesh uses 1 time zone: Asia/Dhaka. The primary time zone is Asia/Dhaka (+06, +06:00).

What is the current time in Dhaka?

The current time in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is approximately 11:54:21 PM (+06:00). Dhaka is in the Asia/Dhaka time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Bangladesh observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Bangladesh does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
