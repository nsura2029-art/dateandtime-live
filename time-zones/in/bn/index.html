Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Brunei have?

Brunei uses 1 time zone: Asia/Brunei. The primary time zone is Asia/Brunei (+08, +08:00).

What is the current time in Bandar Seri Begawan?

The current time in Bandar Seri Begawan, the capital of Brunei, is approximately 1:54:31 AM (+08:00). Bandar Seri Begawan is in the Asia/Brunei time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Brunei observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Brunei does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
