Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Chile have?

Chile uses 3 time zones: America/Coyhaique, America/Punta_Arenas, America/Santiago. The primary time zone is America/Coyhaique (-03, -03:00).

What is the current time in Santiago?

The current time in Santiago, the capital of Chile, is approximately 1:54:23 PM (-04:00). Santiago is in the America/Coyhaique time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Chile observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Chile does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
