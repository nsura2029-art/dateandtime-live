Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Bolivia have?

Bolivia uses 1 time zone: America/La_Paz. The primary time zone is America/La_Paz (-04, -04:00).

What is the current time in Sucre?

The current time in Sucre, the capital of Bolivia, is approximately 1:54:38 PM (-04:00). Sucre is in the America/La_Paz time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Bolivia observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Bolivia does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
