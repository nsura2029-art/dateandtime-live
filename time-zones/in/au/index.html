Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Australia have?

Australia uses 8 time zones: Australia/Adelaide, Australia/Brisbane, Australia/Broken_Hill, Australia/Darwin, Australia/Hobart, Australia/Melbourne, Australia/Perth, Australia/Sydney. The primary time zone is Australia/Adelaide (ACST, +09:30).

What is the current time in Canberra?

The current time in Canberra, the capital of Australia, is approximately 3:54:17 AM (+10:00). Canberra is in the Australia/Adelaide time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Australia observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Australia does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
