Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Azerbaijan have?

Azerbaijan uses 1 time zone: Asia/Baku. The primary time zone is Asia/Baku (+04, +04:00).

What is the current time in Baku?

The current time in Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, is approximately 9:49:35 PM (+04:00). Baku is in the Asia/Baku time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Azerbaijan observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Azerbaijan does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
