About South Africa

Republic of South Africa is located in AF (Africa). The country spans 1,221,037 km² and has a population of approximately 47,939,803 across 304 tracked cities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does South Africa have?

South Africa uses 1 time zone: Africa/Johannesburg. The primary time zone is Africa/Johannesburg (SAST, +02:00).

What is the current time in Pretoria?

The current time in Pretoria, the capital of South Africa, is approximately 7:49:26 PM (+02:00). Pretoria is in the Africa/Johannesburg time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does South Africa observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, South Africa does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
