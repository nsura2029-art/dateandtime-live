Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does China have?

China uses 3 time zones: Asia/Macau, Asia/Shanghai, Asia/Urumqi. The primary time zone is Asia/Macau (CST, +08:00).

What is the current time in Beijing?

The current time in Beijing, the capital of China, is approximately 1:54:17 AM (+08:00). Beijing is in the Asia/Macau time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does China observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, China does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
