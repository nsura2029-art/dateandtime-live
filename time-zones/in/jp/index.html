Frequently Asked Questions

How many time zones does Japan have?

Japan uses 1 time zone: Asia/Tokyo. The primary time zone is Asia/Tokyo (JST, +09:00).

What is the current time in Tokyo?

The current time in Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is approximately 2:54:17 AM (+09:00). Tokyo is in the Asia/Tokyo time zone. Use our live world clock to see the current time in any city.

Does Japan observe Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

No, Japan does not observe Daylight Saving Time. The country stays on a single UTC offset year-round.
