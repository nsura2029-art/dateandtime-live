FAQ

Frequently asked questions

All the questions our users (and Google) ask most often.

What time is it in my city?

dateandtime.live auto-detects your city and shows the current time in the big clock. You can also search any of 33,945 cities worldwide. The clock updates every second and shows the IANA time zone, UTC offset, and DST status.

What time is it in Tokyo right now?

Tokyo (JST, UTC+9) does not observe daylight saving time. The current time is shown in the "Today on Earth" strip on this page. For full details, visit the Japan time zone page.

What time zone is New York in?

New York uses the IANA identifier America/New_York. In winter it's Eastern Standard Time (EST, UTC-5); during daylight saving (mid-March to early November) it's Eastern Daylight Time (EDT, UTC-4).

Is daylight saving time active right now?

Yes. DST is currently active in most of North America, the European Union, and parts of the Middle East. It will end on Sunday, November 2, 2026 in the US and EU. See the global DST schedule for all regions.

When does DST end in 2026?

DST ends on Sunday, November 2, 2026 in the United States, Canada, Mexico (most regions), and the European Union. Clocks fall back one hour at 2:00 AM local time.

What is UTC?

UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) is the primary time standard by which the world regulates clocks and time. It is within about 1 second of mean solar time at 0° longitude and replaced GMT in 1967 as the official global reference.

How do I find a meeting time across time zones?

Use the Meeting Planner. Add the cities of all attendees to the favorites rail, then drag-select a time window to see each person's local time. The tool highlights overlapping working hours (typically 9 AM – 6 PM) for every attendee.

How many time zones are there in the world?

408 IANA time zones in the dateandtime.live database, covering 33,945 cities across 242 countries and territories. The exact number changes as regions adopt or abolish DST or change their boundaries.
